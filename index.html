About me:

My name is Ebba Celor and I'm 19 years old. I live and have grown up in
Sweden. I'm currently studying New Media Design (NMD) in Jönköping
University (JU). The reason I started to study NMD is because I like to be
creative and like to draw on my spare time. I have liked to draw for as long
as I can remember and it has been my favorit subject since I was little,
therefore NMD seemed to be a good option, even if it has a lot of courses
that doesn't include drawing. Besides studying I also work part time at Postnord,
when I have the time.

On my spare time I like to draw, read, listen to music/podcasts,
watch movies/series, play videogames and hang out with friends.
I'm not currently playing any sport but I used to play tennis before, but not anymore.
Among my favorit sports tennis is still one of my favorits and don't mind playing it
from time to time.
